1. Objective
The goal of the game is to test playersâ€™ knowledge of the Final Fantasy series.
Players will answer 20 questions, each with four possible answers, but only one is correct.

2. Game Flow
The game consists of 20 rounds.
In each round, the player receives one question and four answer options: A, B, C, D
The player must select one answer for each question.

3. Scoring
Correct answer: +1 point
Incorrect answer: Game Over
At the end of 20 rounds, the player with the highest score wins.

4. Rules
Each question has only one correct answer.
Players cannot change their answer once submitted.
No external help or devices should be used to answer questions.
In case of a tie, a tiebreaker question may be asked.

5. Tips
Read each question carefully before answering.
Some questions may reference specific games, characters, or story events.
Enjoy the game and test your Final Fantasy knowledge!
